About Us

Our Leadership

Driven by passion and a deep commitment to sustainable farming.

Rooted in Quality, Driven by Passion

Leshole Innovative Integrated Farm is a diversified agricultural enterprise with poultry at its heart. We take pride in raising the finest free-range chickens, alongside healthy cattle, sheep, goats, and seasonal crops — all managed with sustainable, ethical farming practices.

Our integrated approach means nothing goes to waste. Livestock fertilize our fields, crops feed our animals, and the cycle of life on the farm produces exceptional quality for our customers.

📍 Based in Mphanama, South Africa — serving local communities, markets, and beyond with premium farm products.
